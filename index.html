About Me

Welcome to the mind of yours truly. I would like to issue a formal apology to anyone who may or may not be forced to read this. It is not my intention to bore you. I am here to tell you a little bit about myself. I figure that the easiest way to do this is to tell you about What I might be doing if I weren't sitting down at my keyboard writing this very text.

One senario may find me standing 5000ft above sea level, snow falling silently through the the crisp air of Stevens Pass. At this point the only thing I would hear would be my breath and the sound of my own thoughts. I would take one last deep breath before pointing my snowboard downhill. At this point, everything goes quiet. This is the feeling that I strive for, a nearly undescribable feeling. A feeling only acheivable by complete focus.

Another senario may find me at a bar with friends and family. Eating my favorite comfort food while sipping on a new IPA. While I realize that this senario may not be nearly as healthy as the last, there are very few places I would rather be.

On the other hand, maybe I'm right where I should be...
